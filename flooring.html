Wooden flooring


This refers to a type of flooring made from natural wood, engineered wood, or wood composite materials. It is characterized by its natural grain patterns, warmth and durability. The wooden flooring is widely used and suitable for both Residential and Commercial spaces including yoga institution, educational departments or low traffic public buildings.

Rubber flooring


Considering its durability, flexibility and slip-resistance properties, this is the most suitable flooring options for Gymnasium, Sport field, Kids playing area and the Institutions. This is available in various colors, patterns, and customizable designs.

EPDM flooring


We provide high-quality EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) flooring designed for comfort, durability, and style. Perfect for outdoor and indoor spaces, our EPDM floors are weather-resistant, low-maintenance, and customizable to suit any environment. Whether for recreational, industrial, playgrounds, sports areas or commercial use, our EPDM solutions deliver long-lasting performance, vibrant color options, and a sleek, professional look.
