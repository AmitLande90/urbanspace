With high-quality materials and skilled application, we deliver durable solutions that ensure long-lasting enhance the lifespan of your property.

  • UV Resistance
  • Dimensionally stable membrane
  • Good heat resistance.
  • Excellent Waterproofing Characteristics.
  • Good crack bridging property
  • Cold applied and hence does not require heating.
  • Monolithic and Seamless Membrane.
  • Excellent bonding to concrete.
  • Can be applied on moist surface also.
  • Excellent vapour barrier.
  • Good flexibility and does not crack on aging.
